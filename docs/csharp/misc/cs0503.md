 <span data-ttu-id="bb673-104">Il est superflu de marquer une méthode membre à la fois comme [abstract](../../csharp/language-reference/keywords/abstract.md) et [virtual](../../csharp/language-reference/keywords/virtual.md) , car **abstract** implique **virtual**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb673-104">It is redundant to mark a member method as both [abstract](../../csharp/language-reference/keywords/abstract.md) and [virtual](../../csharp/language-reference/keywords/virtual.md) because **abstract** implies **virtual**.</span></span>
